PRODUCT DETAILS



Circuit breaker timer

  • Measure the CLOSE, OPEN & CLOSE-OPEN timings of a circuit breaker.
  • Operate the breaker through the instrument
  • Get the indications for maintenance and overhauling of the circuit breaker
  • Instrument capable of testing circuit breaker timings of upto 765kV circuit breakers
  • Can test 6 breaks of Main and 6 contacts of PIR at a time.

Circuit breaker analyser

  • Measure the CLOSE, OPEN, C-O, O-C, O-C-O timings of a circuit breaker.
  • Measure the timings of auxiliary contacts of circuit breaker
  • Measure and plot the characteristics of breaker coil current
  • Measure and plot the motion/ travel of the main contacts (TRANSDUCERS SUPPLIED)
  • Dynamic Contact Resistance Measurement (DCRM) signature of circuit breakers upto 765kV

Circuit breaker analyser

  • Measure the contact resistance of circuit breaker contacts, isolator joints, welded joints, nuts & bolt contacts etc.
  • Measure the Micro-Ohm value to resistance with the resolution of 0.1 Micro-Ohm.
  • Get the indications of heat dissipation across the contacts
  • Options of 100 A/ 200A current injection is available
  • Option of continuous 100A injection is also available
