PRODUCT DETAILS



Winding Resistance Meter

  • Measure the resistance of transformer windings, motors and rotating machines.
  • Transformers upto 500MVA can be tested.
  • Current injection from 10mA to 50A is available.
  • Resistance range from 0 to 2000 Ohms available.
  • Auto discharge of Back emf generated because of windings of the transformer. Visual and audible indication for back emf discharge.
  • Discharges the winding even if the power to instruments is cut off.

Transformer turns ratio meter

  • Measure the turn ratio.
  • Error percentage calculation.
  • Measure the excitation current.
  • Phase difference measurement.
  • Automatic OLTC control. Measure the parameters at each tap automatically
  • Easy to use , easy to connect instrument
